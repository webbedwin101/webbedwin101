Learning Web Development varies from person to person and really depends on your learning style and how well you can leverage resources to develop the necessary foundational knowledge.

My estimate for anyone who already has introductory experience with a programming language and has a solid technology background is 3-6months. This estimate includes learning the foundational knowledge of Web Development and can be accomplished while working.

Learning Web Development without a solid background in Technology but consistent learning sessions such as a coding bootcamp and self study would take approximately 8-12months.
